About the institute

International Institute of Information Technology, Hyderabad (IIITH) is an autonomous university, founded as not-for-profit public private a partnership (N-PPP) in 1998, and is the first IIIT in India under this model. Over the years, the institute has evolved strong research programmes in various areas, with an emphasis on technology and applied research for industry and society. The institute facilitates interdisciplinary research and a seamless flow of knowledge. Several world-renowned centres of excellence are part of IIITH's research portfolio. It has established various joint collaboration and co-innovation models with an industry outreach spanning significant national and multinational companies.

About the event

Cybersecurity is one of the most critical and challenging domains of the current era. The continuous rise in attacks across various industries has led to significant financial losses and heightened concerns globally. Cybercriminals are employing increasingly sophisticated techniques to exploit vulnerabilities, making this an ever-escalating threat. Addressing this pressing issue requires in-depth discussion and evaluation by experts. This event aims to provide valuable insights into the evolving landscape of cybersecurity, including the technical challenges and strategies employed to mitigate threats. Participants will gain a deeper understanding of how cybersecurity is managed in real-world scenarios, through hands-on experience of cyber security and blockchain technology. By exploring the nature of cybercrimes and prevention techniques, the event seeks to empower attendees with knowledge to combat these threats effectively by providing them hands-on experience.

Objective of the Event

This event aims to discuss and raise awareness in the following areas:

  • Understand the evolving Cybersecurity Landscape
  • Discuss real-world Cybersecurity strategies
  • Role of Blockchain in Cybersecurity
  • Decentralization and Security
  • To gain hands-on experience of Cybersecurity and Blockchain techniques

Themes and Sub-themes

This event aims to discuss and raise awareness in the following areas:

  • Basics of cryptography in cyber security
  • Fortifying Digital Frontiers: Advances in System and Network Security
  • Securing the Future: The Rise of Post-Quantum Cryptography
  • The Digital Battlefield: Combating Cybercrime in the 21st Century
  • Blockchain Innovations: Revolutionizing Industries and Enhancing Securities
  • Decentralized Trust: Exploring the Mechanics of Distributed Blockchain Systems
  • Securing Digital Finances: Emerging Trends in Fintech Cybersecurity
  • Unified Blockchain Framework (UBF) and it's applications
  • The Role of Information Audit and Assurance
  • Federated Learning, Differential Privacy and Blockchains
